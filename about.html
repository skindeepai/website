About PLGL Technology

The story of preference learning in generative latent spaces

SkinDeep.ai Inc

SkinDeep.ai Inc is the pioneering company behind PLGL (Preference Learning in Generative Latent Spaces) technology. Founded with the vision of making AI truly personal, we developed the first system that learns individual preferences to generate personalized content without explicit prompting.

Our breakthrough came from a simple observation: instead of asking users to describe what they want (which is often difficult), why not let them simply rate examples and learn their preferences? This insight led to PLGL - a technology that transforms how humans interact with generative AI.

Our Journey

2018

The Beginning

Started development of preference learning technology using early generative models. Initial experiments showed promising results in learning individual aesthetic preferences.

2019

SkinDeep.ai App Launch

Released the first application demonstrating PLGL technology. Users could discover their "ideal face" through simple swipe interactions. Filed provisional patent for the core methodology.

2020

Technology Refinement

Continued research and development, exploring applications beyond facial generation. Discovered the technology's potential across multiple domains.

2025

Open Source Release

Decided to open source PLGL technology for community benefit. Released comprehensive documentation, code libraries, and examples to enable widespread adoption.

The Philosophy Behind Open Source

Why Open Source? Steve believes in the power of open innovation. By making PLGL open source, the technology becomes defensible through community adoption rather than legal barriers. This aligns with Steve's track record - VDO.Ninja's success came from being open and accessible to all.

Continuous Innovation: Steve's philosophy centers on the belief that there are always new opportunities ahead. Open sourcing PLGL ensures the core ideas remain accessible while creating space for continuous innovation. Agency and creative thought provide endless entry points to build new value.

Building for Impact: From scaling systems to handle hundreds of terabytes at VDO.Ninja, to pioneering browser-based studios at Stage TEN before society needed them, Steve has consistently built technology ahead of its time. PLGL follows this pattern - solving tomorrow's problems today.

"The best moat is continuous innovation and serving real needs."

Our Mission

At SkinDeep.ai Inc, we believe AI should adapt to people, not the other way around. Our mission is to make AI generation as intuitive as expressing a preference - no technical knowledge, no complex prompts, just natural interaction.

By open sourcing PLGL, we aim to democratize personalized AI generation and enable developers worldwide to build applications that truly understand individual preferences.

The Technology

PLGL represents a fundamental shift in how we interact with generative AI:

  • No Prompting Required: Users don't need to describe what they want
  • Universal Application: Works with any generative model with a latent space
  • Privacy-Preserving: Can operate without sharing personal data
  • Continuous Learning: Improves with each interaction
